About Our Teacher

Mr. Alex Rivera has been inspiring students for over a decade with his passion for mathematics and storytelling. Every morning, he greets the class with a quirky puzzle, turning complex algebra into an exciting adventure. His classroom walls are adorned with hand‑drawn fractals and vintage posters of famous mathematicians, creating a space where curiosity thrives.

Beyond the chalkboard, Alex volunteers at local youth coding camps, teaching kids how to turn ideas into interactive games. He believes that learning should be playful, and his students often leave with not only a deeper understanding of equations but also a newfound confidence to tackle any problem.
